Lego’s strategy focuses on consumers by linking creativity and business (Novoseltseva, 2017). Its known as Lego’s Shared Vision, it is a co-invented open innovation model initiative that gives consumers the opportunity to create their personalized Lego sets either with 3D computer applications or Lego bricks through a website

This puts consumers in the center of the innovative project and assists the team with targeting sets which can bring success, it also reduces the risk of wrong innovations

LEGO was able to connect with new market segments through its widespread physical footprint and brand awareness

A very vital innovative step taking by Lego is partnering with MIT Media Lab to deliver computerized bricks which was launched as LEGO Windstorm
